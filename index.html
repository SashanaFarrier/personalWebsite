About Sashana

During my teenage years, I was set on becoming a lawyer. So in high school, I did a course in pre-law studies (while also completing an Associate’s Degree in General Studies) and earned a Diploma at the end of 2 years. My career did not take me where I wanted (bummer!) so I ended up studying Business Administration as a back up plan.

My interest in coding began in 2020 when I decided to try building a website from scratch. Fast-forward today, I got my first Software Engineer internship in August 2023 through the Microsoft Leap as a Service Programme.

Most of my years learning has been spent on the frontend but my main focus these days is developing my backend skills.

When I’m not coding, I’m usually watching Kdrama, playing video games or hanging out with my husband watching anime together.
